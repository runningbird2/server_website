Privacy policy

Effective date: 2026-01-11. This policy applies to the FabricCraft website only and does not cover the Minecraft server or community platforms.

1. Scope

This policy describes how FabricCraft collects and uses information when you visit our website at fabriccraft.net and its linked pages.

2. Information we collect

  • Information you provide: If you email us, we receive your email address, message content, and any attachments you choose to send.
  • Automatic data: Our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) may process standard web logs such as IP address, user agent, referrer, request time, and pages requested for security and performance.
  • Not collected: We do not collect payment information or in-game data through this website.

3. How we use information

  • Respond to inquiries and provide support.
  • Operate, maintain, and secure the website.
  • Troubleshoot issues and prevent abuse.

4. Legal bases (where applicable)

  • Legitimate interests: Running and securing the website.
  • Consent: When you contact us and choose to share information.
  • Legal obligations: When we must comply with applicable laws.

5. Sharing and disclosures

  • We share data with our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) and email service providers only as needed to operate the site.
  • We do not sell personal data or share it for advertising.
  • We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the site and its users.

6. Cookies and tracking

We do not set analytics or advertising cookies. GitHub Pages may set essential cookies for security or performance purposes.

7. Data retention

We keep support emails only as long as necessary to respond and resolve the issue. Technical logs are retained according to our hosting provider's defaults.

8. International transfers

Our hosting and email providers may process data in countries other than your own, including the United States.

9. Your rights and choices

  • You can request access, correction, or deletion of your information.
  • You can withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.
  • You may have additional rights under local law (such as the EEA/UK GDPR or state privacy laws).

10. Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

11. Children's privacy

The website is not intended for children under 13. If we learn we collected personal data from a child, we will delete it.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page.

13. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, email support@fabriccraft.net.
