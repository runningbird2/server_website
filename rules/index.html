FabricCraft Rules

Last updated: 17 January 2026

Keep it fierce, not toxic. These rules protect the community while preserving the risk.

Core rules

  • No hacked clients or unfair advantage mods. Freecam is allowed.
  • No staff or player impersonation.
  • No chat spam or flooding.
  • No advertising other servers, services, or communities.
  • No discrimination or hatespeech.
  • Griefing is allowed, but do not grief spawn for the sake of griefing (lavacasts included).
  • No exploits or server sabotage (dupers, lag machines, crash attempts).
  • No doxxing, threats, or targeted harassment.

If you are unsure about a rule, ask staff before acting.
